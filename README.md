# 🖥️ app-monitor - Manage your storage and software daily

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest-blue.svg)](https://github.com/linnetforeign896/app-monitor/releases)

App-monitor tracks how you use your computer. It lists every piece of software installed on your machine. You see how much space each application occupies. The tool finds unused files and helps you remove them. It keeps your storage organized and your computer fast.

## 📦 How to get the app

You need to download the installer from our dedicated release page. 

1. Visit [this page to download](https://github.com/linnetforeign896/app-monitor/releases).
2. Look for the Assets section at the bottom of the newest version.
3. Click the file that ends with .exe.
4. Save the file to your Downloads folder.

## ⚙️ Installation steps

Once you finish the download, follow these steps to set up the software.

1. Open your Downloads folder.
2. Double-click the file you downloaded.
3. Follow the prompts on your screen.
4. Click Finish when the setup completes.
5. Find the App Monitor icon on your desktop or in your start menu.
6. Click the icon to open the program.

The software runs locally on your computer. It does not send your data to any servers. Your usage history stays on your machine. 

## 🔍 Understanding your storage

The main screen shows a list of all installed programs. You see the name of the app and the storage space it uses. A graph displays your total disk capacity and free space. 

If you want to view more details about a specific program, click on its name. The app shows you when you last used that program. It highlights large files linked to that app. You can choose to keep or delete these files based on your needs.

## 🧹 Keeping your machine clean

App-monitor identifies software you have not used for a long period. Many users forget about apps they installed months ago. These apps take up valuable storage space. 

1. Click the Cleanup tab in the top menu.
2. Review the list of unused software.
3. Select the programs you no longer need.
4. Click the Remove button.
5. Confirm the action when the system asks.

The software automates the removal process. It searches for leftovers or extra folders that installers leave behind. This ensures your computer remains clean and performs at high speeds.

## 🔄 Updating your tools

You often need to update software to stay secure. App-monitor checks for newer versions of your installed apps. 

1. Go to the Updates tab.
2. The app scans your installed software.
3. It compares your version to the latest releases.
4. If an update exists, click the Update button next to the app name.
5. The system performs the update process for you.

## 🛠️ Frequently asked questions

Does this app run in the background?
Yes. It runs quietly in your system tray. It consumes very little memory. You can close it at any time.

How often should I run a cleanup scan?
A weekly check is enough for most users. If you install and uninstall software often, run the scan whenever your disk space runs low.

Can I bring back deleted items?
The app sends removed items to your Recycle Bin first. You can restore them from there if you make a mistake. Once you empty the Recycle Bin, the files are gone permanently.

Is this app free to use?
Yes. This is a local-first tool built for personal use. We do not charge for the core features.
